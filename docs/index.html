Privacy Policy — ua-mcp

Last updated: 2026-07-02

What data we collect

  • Email address and licence key, for Pro tier billing and account management.
  • Payment details are handled entirely by Stripe — we never see or store card information.

How your data is used

  • Free tier tools (ua_status, ua_scan, ua_graph_summary, ua_explain, ua_onboarding_doc) run entirely on your local machine and never contact our servers.
  • The following tools send your project's codebase graph — file paths and import/dependency relationships only, never source code content — to our backend for processing: ua_find_callers, ua_impact_analysis, ua_rules, ua_ci_check, ua_validate_graph.
  • ua_precheck (Free and Pro): sends graph metadata for blast-radius calculation. Processed in-memory only, never stored.
  • This graph data is processed in-memory for the duration of the request only. It is never stored, logged, or persisted.

Where data is stored

  • Licence keys, email addresses, and usage/quota counters are stored in our Supabase database.
  • Billing is handled by Stripe (see stripe.com/privacy).

Third parties we use

  • Stripe (payments), Supabase (database), Render (backend hosting). We do not sell or share your data with anyone else.

Your rights

  • Request deletion of your account data at any time by contacting uaprotocol@gmail.com.
